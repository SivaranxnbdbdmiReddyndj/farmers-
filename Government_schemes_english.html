Government Schemes for Agriculture in Tamil Nadu

Tamil Nadu Mission on Sustainable Dryland Development (TNMSDD) (Phase II)

Description: The Tamil Nadu Mission on Sustainable Dryland Development (TNMSDD) aims to improve the productivity and sustainability of dryland agriculture. The scheme focuses on enhancing soil health, water conservation, and crop management practices.

Usefulness for Farmers:

  • Soil and Water Management: Provides techniques to improve soil health and water use efficiency, crucial for dryland areas.
  • Increased Productivity: Helps increase crop yields through sustainable practices and innovative technologies.
  • Resilience to Climate Change: Enhances the resilience of farming systems to climatic variability.

Uniqueness: Integrates modern technologies and practices tailored for dryland farming, addressing specific challenges faced by farmers in these regions. Focuses on a holistic approach combining soil health, water conservation, and crop management.
